🔧 5. When Things Go Wrong (Click the Symptom)

This is what Stratos does — but you need to understand the framework too. Click the symptom you're seeing:

Diagnosis: Creative Problem

Your ads aren't interesting enough to stop the scroll. The algorithm is showing them but people keep scrolling past. Action: Add 20+ more diverse creatives. Not variations of the same ad — completely different angles, different visuals, different hooks. The algorithm needs variety to find what resonates.

Diagnosis: Landing Page Problem

The ad is doing its job — people are interested and clicking. But when they land on the page, something kills the conversion. Could be: slow load time, confusing form, mismatched messaging, too many steps. Action: Fix the landing page. This might not be your job — escalate to whoever built the page.

Diagnosis: Follow-Up Problem

The ads are working. The landing page is working. But the leads go cold between form submission and the appointment. Action: Fix the nurture sequence. They need faster follow-up (text within 5 minutes), reminder emails, and confirmation messages. This is an automation/operations problem, not an ads problem.

Diagnosis: Sales Problem

Everything upstream is working perfectly. The ads bring leads, they show up — but the client can't close them. This is NOT your problem. Your job ends at delivering qualified leads. If the client can't close, that's a sales training issue. Document the data and show them the leads are quality.

Diagnosis: Creative Fatigue or Algorithm Reset

Two possibilities: (1) Creative fatigue — your audience has seen the same ads too many times. Solution: add 20 new diverse creatives. (2) Algorithm reset — sometimes Meta's system recalibrates. Solution: wait 72 hours. If CPMs don't normalize, it's fatigue — feed it fresh creative.

🔴 The golden rule: When in doubt, add more diverse creatives. 90% of performance problems in the Andromeda era are creative problems. Not targeting. Not budget. Not placements. Creative diversity.
