Lesson 4.2 — Module 4: Scaling

Advanced Reporting — Monthly Strategy Calls

Reporting Strategy Calls ~15 min
You already know how to send weekly reports (lesson 3.11). Now it's time to level up: monthly strategy calls that make clients feel like they have a CMO on their team. This is how you retain clients for 12+ months — and upsell them.

📊 1. The Monthly Report (Goes Deeper Than Weekly)

Your weekly report is a quick snapshot. The monthly report tells the story of the month — what happened, why it happened, and what you're doing next. Send this 1 day before the monthly call.

📋 Monthly Report Template
📊 MONTHLY PERFORMANCE REPORT — [Month Year] [Business Name] | Prepared by [Your Name] ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ 📈 THE BIG NUMBERS • Total Ad Spend: $[X] • Total Leads Generated: [X] • Cost Per Lead: $[X] • Estimated Revenue from Ads: $[X] • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): [X]x 📊 MONTH-OVER-MONTH COMPARISON This Month Last Month Change Spend: $[X] $[X] [+/-X%] Leads: [X] [X] [+/-X%] CPL: $[X] $[X] [+/-X%] Revenue: $[X] $[X] [+/-X%] 🏆 WINS THIS MONTH • [Best performing ad/angle and why] • [Any record days or weeks] • [New audience or creative that worked] 📉 CHALLENGES • [What didn't work and what you learned] • [Any external factors — seasonality, competition] 🎯 NEXT MONTH PLAN • [3 specific things you'll do differently] • [New creative angles to test] • [Budget recommendation] 💡 RECOMMENDATION [One clear recommendation — increase budget, add service, etc.]

📞 2. The 30-Minute Monthly Strategy Call

This call is the single most important thing for client retention. It's where you go from "vendor" to "partner." Here's the exact structure:

Minutes 1-10: The Numbers
⏱️ 10 min
Walk through the monthly report. Spend, leads, CPL, revenue. Compare to last month. Keep it simple — don't bury them in data. Lead with the number THEY care about (usually leads or revenue, never impressions).
Minutes 10-20: The Learnings
⏱️ 10 min
What worked, what didn't, and what you learned. Show your top performing ad and explain WHY it worked. This is where you demonstrate expertise. "The family angle outperformed the discount angle 3:1 — your customers respond more to emotion than price."
Minutes 20-30: Next Month Plan
⏱️ 10 min
3 specific things you'll do next month. New creative angles, budget changes, new offers to test. Ask for their input — "Any promotions coming up? Seasonal events? New menu items?" This makes them feel heard and gives you content ideas.

📉 3. How to Present Bad Months

Bad months happen. CPL spikes, leads drop, a campaign tanks. How you handle it determines whether the client stays or leaves.

🚫 NEVER DO THIS:
"The algorithm was being weird this month"
"Facebook is just really competitive right now"
"I'm not sure what happened"
Hiding or spinning the numbers
✅ THE FORMULA: Own It → Explain Why → Show the Plan
"Hey Tony, I want to be upfront — this month wasn't as strong as last month. CPL went from $12 to $18. Here's what happened: our top 3 creatives hit creative fatigue around week 3, and I didn't refresh them fast enough. That's on me. Here's what I'm doing about it: I've already built 10 new ad variations, and I'm testing a completely new angle — customer testimonial videos. Based on what I'm seeing in the first 48 hours, next month should bounce back. I'll have an update for you by next Friday."
Clients don't expect perfection. They expect honesty and a plan. This script keeps them.

🧮 4. The ROI Calculator (Speak Their Language)

Most clients don't understand ROAS or CPL. They understand: "I put in X dollars and got Y dollars back." Show them that math every month.

🖥️ SHOW ON SCREEN: Build this calculation live for Tony's Pizza during the call. Use their real numbers. The "wow" moment is when they see the profit number.
🍕 Example: Tony's Pizza — January
Ad Spend -$1,500
Your Management Fee -$1,500
Total Investment -$3,000
Leads Generated 125 leads
Avg Order Value $35
Close Rate (est. 40%) 50 new customers
Revenue from Ads +$1,750
Repeat Customer Revenue (est. 3x/year) +$5,250
Total Customer Value (Year 1) +$7,000
ROI: 133% — For every $1 spent, Tony gets $2.33 back

Pro tip: Always include the repeat customer math. First-time orders undervalue your work. When Tony sees that 50 new customers = $7K+ in yearly revenue for a $3K investment, he'll never cancel.

💎 5. Upsell Opportunities During Monthly Calls

Monthly calls are the best time to grow your revenue per client. Don't "sell" — just make natural suggestions based on results.

💰 Budget Increase
"We're getting leads at $12 each. If we increase budget from $1.5K to $2.5K, we could get 80 more leads next month. Want to test it?"
📱 Add Instagram
"Your Facebook ads are crushing it. I think we could get even better results on Instagram with video content. Want me to add that for an extra $500/mo?"
🔄 Retargeting Campaign
"We have 2,000 website visitors from ads now. I can run a retargeting campaign to bring back the ones who didn't convert. Usually $300-500/mo extra."
📍 Google Ads
"People are searching for 'pizza near me' 5,000 times a month in your area. We could add Google Ads to catch those. Extra $1K/mo management."
💡 Only upsell when results are GOOD. Never try to upsell during a bad month. Wait until you have wins to build on.
