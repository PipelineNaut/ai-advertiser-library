Lesson 4.4 — Module 4: Scaling

Hiring Your First VA — Buy Back Your Time

Delegation Hiring ~15 min
At 4-5 clients, you'll hit a wall. You're spending 5+ hours per day on repetitive tasks. A Virtual Assistant (VA) at $5-8/hour can handle 60% of that work. This lesson shows you exactly who to hire, what to delegate, and how to train them in one weekend.

⏰ 1. When to Hire

⚠️ Signs You Need a VA:
✓ You have 4+ clients
✓ You're spending 5+ hours/day on client work
✓ You're turning down new business because of time
✓ You're doing tasks that don't require YOUR brain (uploading, formatting, screenshots)
✓ You're making $6K+/month (you can afford the $800-1,200/mo cost)
💰 The Math That Makes It Obvious
WITHOUT VA
5 clients × $1,500 = $7,500/mo
Working 5-6 hours/day
No time for new clients
Stuck at $7,500
WITH VA ($1,000/mo)
7 clients × $1,500 = $10,500/mo
Working 2-3 hours/day
VA handles 15+ hours/week
Net: $9,500/mo (and growing)

📋 2. What to Delegate (and What NOT To)

✅ DELEGATE (Day 1)
📸 Screenshot collection from Ads Manager
📊 Report formatting (fill in the template)
🎨 Creative uploads to Ads Manager
📅 Scheduling client calls
📂 Organizing Google Drive files
🔍 Competitor research (save ads to folder)
📧 Basic email follow-ups (from templates)
🚫 NEVER DELEGATE
💬 Client communication (calls, strategy)
🧠 Campaign strategy decisions
⚙️ Optimization decisions (kill/scale)
💰 Budget changes
📞 Sales calls with prospects
🤝 Monthly strategy calls
📝 Writing ad copy (you + AI do this)

The rule: If a task requires judgment, keep it. If it's repetitive and can be taught with a Loom video, delegate it. Your VA handles the HANDS. You keep the BRAIN.

🎓 4. How to Train Your VA (One Weekend)

Training is simple: record yourself doing the task, then let them copy you. Here's the exact process:

1
Record Loom videos for every task
Screen record yourself doing the task from start to finish. Narrate what you're doing and WHY. 5-10 min per video. Loom is free →
2
Write a simple SOP doc
Google Doc with numbered steps + Loom link. Nothing fancy. Just: "Step 1: Open Ads Manager. Step 2: Click on [client name]. Step 3: Screenshot the dashboard..."
3
Have them do the task while you watch
First time: they share their screen and do it live. You correct mistakes in real-time. Takes 30 min.
4
Review their work for 2 weeks, then trust the process
Check their output daily for the first 2 weeks. After that, spot-check weekly. Good VAs become autonomous fast.
🖥️ SHOW ON SCREEN: Open Loom, record yourself taking a dashboard screenshot in Ads Manager. Show how easy it is to create a training video. This is your first SOP.
