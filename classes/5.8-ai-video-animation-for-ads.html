Lesson 5.8 — Module 5: AI Integration

AI Video & Animation for Ads

Video Creative ~7 min read
Video ads almost always outperform static images on Meta — higher engagement, lower CPM, more conversions. You don't need a video editor or After Effects. AI tools make video ads in minutes. Text animations, product showcases, short clips with motion graphics — stuff that used to cost $500 from a freelancer.

🛠️ The Tools

💻
Remotion
ADVANCED — CODE-BASED
Create videos with code. Tell Claude "build me a Remotion video with these 3 testimonials" and it writes the code. Best for templated videos you'll reuse — weekly reports, dynamic text overlays.
🎬
Runway
RECOMMENDED — EASIEST
Most popular AI video tool. Give it an image or text prompt and it generates a short video clip. Best for turning your AI images into video with subtle motion.
🎥
Kling
LONGER CLIPS
Handles longer clips and complex motion better. Best for 10-15 second scenes with people moving — someone walking into a gym, a car driving up.
✨
Pika
QUICK & FUN
Quick animations, fun effects. Best for product photo effects, logo animations, "3D pop" style visuals that do well on Instagram.

💻 Remotion: Tell Claude What to Build

Set up a Remotion project, then just describe what you want:

npx create-video@latest my-ad-video cd my-ad-video npm start

Then tell Claude:

i have a Remotion project set up. create a 15-second vertical video (1080x1920) with: - a bold headline that animates in from the bottom: "Still Paying $200/mo for a Gym You Never Go To?" - hold for 3 seconds - transition to second text: "Try Us Free for 30 Days" - hold for 2 seconds - end card with a colored background and text: "Link in Bio" use a dark background with white text. modern clean font.

Claude writes the code. You run it. You get an MP4. No video editor needed. Build the template once, swap text for different clients.

🎬 Runway: Image to Video

  1. Go to runway.com, sign up (free trial available)
  2. Upload one of your AI-generated ad images from lesson 5.7
  3. Select "Image to Video"
  4. Add a motion prompt like "slow zoom in, hair blowing slightly in wind"
  5. Generate — you get a 4-10 second clip

💡 Example: Generate a static image of a pizza being pulled from an oven (5.7 prompts), upload to Runway, prompt "slow pull back, steam rising from pizza, warm flickering light." That 5 seconds of motion makes the ad feel way more premium than a static image.

🔄 The Full Workflow for Client Video Ads

  • Write ad copy using your copywriter agent (Lesson 5.6)
  • Generate the base image using AI (Lesson 5.7)
  • Pick your video tool: subtle motion → Runway, people moving → Kling, text animations → Remotion, quick social → Pika
  • Add text overlays in the video tool or bring the clip into Canva Video (free)
  • Export as MP4 — 1080×1920 for stories/reels, 1080×1080 for feed
  • Upload to Meta Ads Manager

Total time: 15-30 minutes for a finished video ad. No designer. No editor. No freelancer.

⚡ Quick Start: Your First AI Video Ad

Want to get one done right now? Takes about 10 minutes:

  1. Go to ChatGPT, generate an ad image (use prompts from 5.7)
  2. Go to runway.com, upload that image
  3. Set motion to "slow zoom in with slight camera movement"
  4. Download the 5-second clip
  5. Open Canva, create a new "Mobile Video" project
  6. Drop the clip in, add headline text and CTA on top
  7. Export as MP4

You just made a video ad. 10 minutes. No designer.
