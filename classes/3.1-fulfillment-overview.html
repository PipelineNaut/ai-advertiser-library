Lesson 3.1 — Module 3: Fulfillment

Fulfillment Overview

Fulfillment ~5 min
The client just said yes. Now what? This lesson maps the complete pipeline from signed deal to first ad live — and gives you a checklist you'll use for every single client you onboard.

⚡ 1. The 7-Day Launch Pipeline

Every new client follows the same path. Seven days from "yes" to live ads. No guesswork, no improvising. You follow the system.

Day 0 — Close
Client signs contract + pays first invoice
Send contract (DocuSign or PandaDoc). Collect first payment via Stripe. Send welcome email with onboarding form.
Day 1 — Onboarding Call
30-min kickoff call + access collection
Introduce yourself properly. Walk through the onboarding form together. Collect all access (Meta Business Manager, website, Google Analytics). Set expectations: "You'll have ads live within 7 days."
Day 2 — Research + Setup
Ad account setup + pixel installation + research
Set up or audit their Meta ad account. Install the pixel on their website. Research their competitors, audience, and local market using the AI agents.
Day 3–4 — Creative Production
AI generates ad copy + creatives + audiences
Use the AI Ad Copy Agent to generate 10+ variations. Use the AI Creative Agent to build image/video concepts. Use the AI Audience Agent to find targeting. Send the creative list to the client for approval or recording.
Day 5–6 — Campaign Build
Build campaigns in Ads Manager + QA
Structure campaigns (CBO or ABO depending on budget). Upload all creatives. Set budgets. Double-check targeting, placements, pixel events. Run through QA checklist before launching.
Day 7 — Launch
Ads go live + client notification
Hit publish. Send the client a message: "Your ads are live. Here's what to expect in the first 48 hours." Set up the AI Optimizer to start monitoring.

A client paying $2K/month for 12 months is $24K. That same client churning after month 1 is $2K and a bad review. The difference is fulfillment. Follow the checklist below for every client and you'll keep them for the long haul.

🤝 3. Your Job vs. The Agents' Job

You Handle

  • Client communication + relationship
  • Onboarding call + access collection
  • Approving AI-generated creatives
  • Sending weekly reports
  • Monthly strategy calls
  • Upselling + renewals

AI Agents Handle

  • Ad copy generation (10+ variations)
  • Creative concepts by niche
  • Audience research + targeting
  • Campaign uploads + button clicking
  • Performance monitoring
  • Killing underperformers automatically

You are the face. The agents are the engine. The client never needs to know how the sausage is made — they just need results and communication.
