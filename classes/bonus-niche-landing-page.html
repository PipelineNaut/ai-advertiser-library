Copy this entire prompt. Replace everything in [BRACKETS] with your prospect's details. Paste into Claude. That's it.

Build me a single-page HTML/CSS landing page for my AI advertising agency. I'm targeting [NICHE — e.g. "plumbing businesses"] in [CITY/REGION]. The prospect I'm reaching is: [BUSINESS NAME] The page should: 1. HEADLINE: Lead with their specific pain point, e.g. "Are You Still Relying on Word-of-Mouth to Get [Niche] Jobs?" — then hit the dream outcome in the subheadline 2. SECTION — What We Do: Explain that we run AI-powered Meta ads specifically for [niche] businesses. Use their industry language. Mention the AI Arbitrage System as the mechanism (AI builds creatives, writes copy, optimizes daily — no guessing). 3. SECTION — Results We Get: 2-3 bullet points with specific outcomes a [niche] business would care about (cost per lead, booked appointments, ROAS). Use realistic ranges, not fake specifics. 4. SECTION — How It Works: 3-step process - Step 1: Discovery call (we learn your business) - Step 2: We build & launch AI-powered campaigns in 7 days - Step 3: Ongoing optimization — we only win when you win 5. SECTION — About Us: 1-2 sentences. "We're a performance marketing agency that works exclusively with [niche] businesses. We use AI to do what traditional agencies charge $5K/month for — at a fraction of the cost." 6. CTA: Big button — "Book a Free Strategy Call" — links to [YOUR CALENDLY LINK] Design rules: - Dark background (#0A0A0A) - White text, blue accent (#3B82F6) - Clean, minimal, premium feel - Mobile responsive - No images (text only — clean and fast-loading) - Single HTML file with inline CSS (no external dependencies) Output ONLY the complete HTML file. Nothing else. No explanation. Start with <!DOCTYPE html>

🚀 6. Deploy to Vercel in 60 Seconds

1
Save the HTML file
Save Claude's output as index.html on your computer
2
Create a GitHub repo
Go to github.com/new → Name it something like plumbing-ads → Upload your index.html → Commit
3
Deploy on Vercel
Go to vercel.com → New Project → Import your GitHub repo → Deploy. Takes 30 seconds. You get a live URL immediately: yourproject.vercel.app
4
Send it in your outreach
When a prospect asks for more info, send: "Here's our page specifically for [niche] businesses: [your vercel URL]"
5
(Optional) Connect a custom domain
Buy a domain like plumbingadsagency.com ($12/year on Namecheap). Connect it in Vercel settings → Domain tab. Now it looks like a real business, not a test project.

🧑‍💼 Bonus: Build YOUR Own Authority Page (No Case Studies Needed)

Before you start sending outreach, you need your own page. Not a client page — a page about YOU and your agency. This is what prospects find when they Google your name after your DM. If it doesn't exist, you lose. If it's generic, you lose. If it's specific and professional, you win.

🔑
The No-Case-Studies Solution: Most of you are starting from scratch. No results yet. No clients. That's fine — you don't lead with case studies. You lead with the mechanism (AI Arbitrage System), your niche specificity, and your process. That's enough to close calls.

The 7-Section Authority Page Structure

1
Hero — Photo + What You Do + Who You Help
Your photo (real, professional or not), headline: "I Help [NICHE] Businesses in [REGION] Get More [LEADS/CLIENTS/JOBS] with AI-Powered Ads"
CTA: "Book a Free Strategy Call" (Calendly).
Sub-CTA: "No pitch. Just an honest look at what's possible for your business."
2
Who You Work With (Niche Specificity)
"I specialize in [NICHE 1], [NICHE 2], and [NICHE 3] businesses in [REGION]."
3 niche cards with icons. Each shows the niche name + 1-line value prop.
Message: When I know your industry, I can launch in days — not weeks.
3
What I Do — 3-Step Process
Step 1: Discovery Call (30 min) — I learn your business, goals, constraints
Step 2: AI-Powered Campaign Build (7 days) — AI writes copy, builds creatives, targets audience. You approve first.
Step 3: Launch + Optimize — Weekly reporting. I handle everything else.
4
The AI Arbitrage System — Your Credibility Anchor
This replaces case studies. The mechanism IS the credibility.
"AI writes 10+ ad variations and tests them simultaneously. AI identifies your highest-converting audience. AI optimizes daily — killing losers, scaling winners."
Result: better results faster, at lower cost.
5
My Commitment (Exclusivity)
"I only work with [X] clients at a time."
Explain why: you're fully in, monitoring daily, analyzing weekly.
Include: "One client per niche per area."
6
About Me — Real, Human, Honest
Photo + name + 2-3 sentence bio.
Even if you're just starting: "I'm a performance marketing specialist who uses AI to help local businesses get more customers. I work exclusively with [niche] businesses in [region]."
Social links if you have them.
7
Book a Call — Final CTA
"Ready to see what AI ads can do for your business?" + big Calendly button.
Objection handler below the button: "Still not sure? That's fine. Book the call anyway. You'll walk away with at least 3 things you can do immediately to improve your marketing — whether we work together or not."
