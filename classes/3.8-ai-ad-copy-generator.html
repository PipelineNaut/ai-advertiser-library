🚨 5. Spot the AI — Anti-Pattern Detector

AI writes patterns that human brains detect instantly. Your job: catch them and fix them before the ad goes live. Click each pattern to see the before/after:

🖥️ SHOW ON SCREEN: Click through each anti-pattern. Read the BAD version, then the FIXED version. Ask students: "Which one sounds like a person wrote it?"
1. The "Not X — It's Y" Contrast
AI loves dramatic contrasts with em dashes
COMMON
❌ AI VERSION
"This isn't just pizza — it's an experience. It's not delivery — it's a revolution in your kitchen."
✅ HUMAN VERSION
"Real Italian dough. 20-minute delivery. $12.99. Tony's Pizza — order now."
2. The Triple Structure
"X. Y. And Z." — three parallel items in a row
COMMON
❌ AI VERSION
"Fresh ingredients. Fast delivery. Unbeatable prices. That's the Tony's difference."
✅ HUMAN VERSION
"Our dough is made at 5am with imported Italian flour. Your pizza's at the door in 20 minutes. Oh and it's $12.99."
3. The "Imagine This" Opener
"Imagine...", "Picture this...", "What if..."
VERY COMMON
❌ AI VERSION
"Imagine biting into a slice of perfectly crispy, hand-tossed pizza that melts in your mouth..."
✅ HUMAN VERSION
"I took one bite and said out loud — 'this is $12.99?'"
4. Filler Power Words
"Elevate", "Transform", "Unlock", "Game-changer", "Seamless"
INSTANT TELL
❌ AI VERSION
"Elevate your dinner experience with our game-changing pizza that transforms ordinary nights into extraordinary moments."
✅ HUMAN VERSION
"My kids stopped fighting for 10 whole minutes. All it took was a Tony's pepperoni pizza."
5. The Question-Then-Answer
"Tired of X? We've got the solution."
OVERUSED
❌ AI VERSION
"Tired of cold delivery pizza? Looking for something better? Want real Italian quality without the restaurant price? Tony's Pizza has the answer."
✅ HUMAN VERSION
"I used to think $35 for delivery pizza was normal. Then I found Tony's. $12.99 and it's actually better. I'm mad nobody told me sooner."
6. The Perfect Grammar Robot
Every sentence perfectly structured, no contractions, no slang
SUBTLE
❌ AI VERSION
"We are proud to offer Curitiba's finest artisanal pizza, crafted with imported ingredients and delivered to your doorstep within 20 minutes of your order."
✅ HUMAN VERSION
"We've been making pizza in Curitiba for 15 years. Imported Italian flour, San Marzano tomatoes, mozzarella that actually stretches. At your door in 20 min. Just order already."

The rule: Read every AI-generated ad out loud. If you'd cringe hearing a real person say it in conversation, rewrite it. If it sounds like a brochure, rewrite it. If it sounds like every other ad you've ever seen, rewrite it.

📂 6. Build Your Swipe File (This Never Stops)

A swipe file is a collection of ads, hooks, formats, and creative ideas you save from the real world. The best media buyers never start from zero — they pull from a library of proven stuff they've been collecting for months.

🖥️ SHOW ON SCREEN: Open your swipe file folder (Google Drive, Notion, or just a phone folder). Show how you save ads. Open Meta Ad Library and save 2-3 competitor ads live.
🗂️ What Goes in Your Swipe File
📸 Ad Formats
Screenshots of ads that stopped YOUR scroll. Single images, carousels, videos, UGC-style, before/afters, memes, testimonials. Save the format, not just the copy.
🎣 Hooks That Worked
First lines that grabbed you. Screenshot them. Save the pattern, not the exact words. "I was skeptical until..." is a pattern. "I tried X so you don't have to" is a pattern.
🏆 Competitor Ads (60+ days)
From Meta Ad Library — any ad running 60+ days is a proven winner. Save the messaging angle, the visual style, and the CTA. Study WHY it works.
🎬 Video Styles
TikToks, Reels, YouTube ads that made you watch past 3 seconds. Save the structure: talking head, text overlay, POV, unboxing, demo, reaction, story time.
REAL EXAMPLE — Swipe File on Google Drive (12 categories)

12 folders. Every time you see something good — an ad, a VSL, a landing page, an email — screenshot it and drop it in the right folder. Six months from now this is your creative goldmine.

REAL EXAMPLE — Meta Ad Library search for "Pizza Miami"

See those "Started running on" dates? Fradiavolo's ad has been live since September 2025 — that's 5+ months. They wouldn't keep spending if it wasn't working. That's a proven winner. Study the angle, the format, the copy. Save it to your swipe file.

Start today. Never stop. The bigger your swipe file, the faster you can create ads. When a client says "I need 20 new ads," you don't start from scratch — you open your swipe file and adapt proven formats. Six months from now, you'll have hundreds of ideas ready to go. This is your unfair advantage over every media buyer who tries to reinvent the wheel every time.

✂️ 7. The 60-Second Edit Process

Aurelius gives you 12 ads. You need to clean them up before launch. Here's the exact process — takes about 60 seconds per ad:

1
Read Aloud
Would you say this to a friend?
2
Check Hook
First line = scroll stopper?
3
Kill AI Words
Elevate, transform, seamless → delete
4
Add Specifics
"$12.99" beats "affordable"
5
Match Voice
Sound like the brand, not AI

12 ads × 60 seconds = 12 minutes total editing time. That's it. You now have 12 unique, human-sounding ads ready for upload. A traditional copywriter would charge $50-100 per ad. You just did it for free in under 15 minutes total.
