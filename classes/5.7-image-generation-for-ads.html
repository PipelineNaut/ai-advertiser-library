Image Generation for Ads

Creative AI Images ~7 min read
You don't need a graphic designer. AI image generators can make product mockups, lifestyle shots, before/after visuals, and scroll-stopping ad images in minutes. This lesson covers the tools, prompting tricks, and 12 ready-to-use prompts for common niches.

🛠️ Which Tool to Use

🧠

📐 Prompting Rules for Ad Images

  • Be specific about the scene. Not "a person at a gym" but "a 30-year-old woman in a modern gym doing a dumbbell curl, smiling, wearing a black tank top, warm lighting and brick walls."
  • Include the format. Say "square format for Instagram feed" or "vertical 9:16 for stories."
  • Say what style you want. "Professional photo realistic" vs "flat illustration" vs "cinematic lighting" produce completely different results.
  • Mention text placement. "Leave empty space in the top third for text" or "clean background on the left side for copy."
  • No logos in the image. AI is terrible at text and logos. Generate clean and add text/logos in Canva after.

🧪 The Prompting Formula

[subject] in [setting/location], [action/pose], [style/mood], [lighting], [camera angle], [format]. [additional details]. Leave space for text overlay on [position].

A happy family eating pizza at a cozy Italian restaurant, warm golden lighting, shot from slightly above like a food blog photo, photo realistic, square format for Instagram. The restaurant has exposed brick and candles on the table. Leave empty space in the top portion for text overlay.

🎨 After You Generate: The Canva Step

Your AI image is the background. The finished ad needs text:

  • Upload your AI-generated image
  • Add your headline text on top
  • Add a CTA button or text ("Book Now", "Claim Your Free Trial")
  • Drop in the client's logo
  • Export as PNG or JPG

Takes 5 minutes. Professional-looking ad creative that cost you $0 and no designer.

🔄 Before/After Prompts

These work great for results-based businesses (med spas, detailing, landscaping):

Create a side-by-side before and after image. Left side: [describe the "before" state]. Right side: [describe the "after" state]. Clean white divider line in the middle, "BEFORE" and "AFTER" labels at the top. Photo realistic, square format.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

⚠️ Don't put text in the AI image. It will look like gibberish. Always add text in Canva after.

⚠️ Don't use the first image you get. Generate 3-4 versions and pick the best one.

⚠️ Don't forget the format. If you need 1:1 square for feed ads, say it in the prompt. Cropping after always looks worse.

⚠️ Don't make it too perfect. Ads that look "real" outperform stock photos. "Candid shot" and "natural lighting" help a lot.
