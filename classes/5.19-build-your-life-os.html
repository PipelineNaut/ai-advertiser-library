🧭 5. What's Inside Your Life OS

Four core pieces. Each one stays out of your way until you need it.

🏠
Feature 1 · Home
Command Center
Your launch screen. Tells you in 10 seconds how your life is doing today.
  • Balance Wheel — radial chart, 5 spokes, one per area, filled to its 0-100 score. Your signature visual.
  • Life Score — combined 0-100 number always visible in the top bar.
  • What Matters Now — 3 SMART recommendation cards: Top Priority · Biggest Risk · Biggest Opportunity.
  • Domain Tiles — 5 cards, one per area, with the key metric + trend arrow. Click to drill in.
  • This Week — calendar slice with open commitments for the next 7 days.
📂
Feature 2 · Detail
5 Domain Screens
One screen per area. Same template. Click the wheel or a tile to dive in.
  • Score header — 0-100 with target bar showing how far to go.
  • Metrics row — 3-4 key metrics with sparklines showing the trend.
  • Trend chart — your primary metric over time, clean line.
  • Log — recent entries (workouts, calls, decisions, journal).
  • Advisor card — one SMART recommendation specific to that area.
  • Domain extras: Finances has Money Map + P&L sub-tabs; Mind has Journal + Reading list + Considering + Values; Relationships has the People directory with cadence + birthdays.
🤖
Feature 3 · Intelligence
The AI Advisor
Reads your 5 areas plus your values from Mega-Brain. Surfaces what to fix next.
  • Daily briefing — 1 paragraph: where you are, what to do today.
  • SMART recommendations — every suggestion is specific, measurable, deadlined. Nothing vague ever ships.
  • Per-domain advice — each screen gets its own "where you're lacking" call.
  • "Where you're lacking" ranking — ranks the 5 areas by score, jumps you to the weakest.
  • Chat panel — ask anything; it answers grounded in YOUR data + Mega-Brain, not a generic LLM.
✅
Feature 4 · Action
The Tasks System
One click turns any AI suggestion into action. Stays gone once you act.
  • "Create Task" button on every Advisor card → adds it to your Tasks list.
  • Persistent storage — survives refresh, tab close, and tomorrow's session.
  • Sound + animation — quick pop sound + slide-out. You feel the action land.
  • No zombie suggestions — once you Accept or Dismiss, that card never comes back.
  • Monthly P&L auto-task — fires automatically on the 30th of every month so you never forget the books.
  • SMART rule enforced — the system rejects vague suggestions before they reach you.

📅 6. The Exact Daily Routine

The dashboard only works if you run the routine. Five touchpoints — total time ~13 min/day plus a 15-min weekly review and a 30-min monthly close.

☀️
Morning · 5 min · First thing on the laptop
Open the tab. Pick the day's #1.
  1. Open your Life OS tab (it's pinned, so it's already there)
  2. Glance at the Life Score — note any change vs. yesterday
  3. Look at the Balance Wheel — identify the shortest spoke today
  4. Read the AI Advisor briefing (1 paragraph) — that's your context for the day
  5. Pick ONE card from "What Matters Now" → click Create Task
  6. Skim today's commitments in This Week
  7. Close the tab? NO. Leave it open behind your other windows all day.
📝
During the day · glances · ~3 min total
Log it when it happens. Don't batch.
  1. Finished a workout / run → log it in Health (30 sec)
  2. Had a meaningful conversation or call → log in the right domain's Log
  3. New decision came up → drop it in Mind → Considering
  4. New AI recommendation appears → Accept OR Dismiss; don't leave it hanging
  5. Something hits emotionally (good or bad) → 1-line journal entry in Mind
  6. Quick mental check at lunch: how am I tracking on today's #1?
🌙
Night · 5 min · Before bed
Close the loop. Set tomorrow's #1.
  1. Open Mind → Journal → 1-line entry on the day (mood word + 1 sentence)
  2. Open Tasks → check off completed; move incomplete to tomorrow
  3. Update any manual metrics for the day (hours slept planned, training done, etc.)
  4. Set tomorrow's #1 priority — write it as a SMART task with tomorrow's deadline
  5. Glance the Balance Wheel one more time — is any spoke moving the wrong way?
📊
Weekly · Sunday or Friday · 15 min
Review the wheel. Plan the week.
  1. Look at the Balance Wheel — which spoke moved up or down vs. last week?
  2. Update any metrics that need manual entry (weight, training count, MRR)
  3. Open Relationships → People — call (or schedule) anyone past their cadence
  4. Read your Mind → Considering list — close any decision you've been sitting on for 2+ weeks
  5. Pick the week's ONE big priority — add it as a SMART task with Sunday's deadline
  6. If a spoke dropped 5+ points, re-read its Advisor card and create one fix-it task
📆
Monthly · the 30th · 30 min
Close the books. Look for patterns.
  1. Auto-task fires: "Log expenses and close [Month]'s P&L" — open it
  2. Update Finances — net worth pieces, monthly P&L, investment allocation
  3. Take a new Net Worth snapshot — it auto-adds to the trend chart
  4. Read your Journal entries from the last 30 days — look for mood patterns + recurring issues
  5. Re-check Mind → Values — are your actions matching what you said matters?
  6. Adjust targets for next month based on what actually happened (not what you hoped)
  7. Optional: change your password if you've been sharing the screen

🏆 Total time investment: ~13 min/day + 15 min/wk + 30 min/mo. Under 5 hours per month for a system that compounds across 5 areas of your life. There is no other 5-hour investment that comes close.
