🚀 4. The Decision: Comfortable or Scale?

Option A: Stay at $10K
Keep 5-7 clients. Work 3 hours a day. Enjoy the lifestyle. Nothing wrong with this — you built a real business that pays more than most jobs, with a fraction of the hours.
Best for: people who value time freedom above all else.
Option B: Push to $30K
Raise prices. Add more clients. Hire a second VA. Build a small team. Module 5 shows you exactly how — including the AI tools that make it possible without burning out.
Best for: people who want to build an agency, not just a freelance gig.
