If you got hit by a bus tomorrow, your agency dies in 2 weeks. That's the test you're failing. Today we fix it — by turning the processes locked in your head into ClickUp SOPs in ~40 min each (instead of the 3 hours it used to take). Watch this once. Walk out with one SOP done.

🚌 1. The Bus Test — Are You the Owner or the Employee?

The Bus Test

"If you got hit by a bus tomorrow, would your agency still run for 30 days? 60? A year?" Most owners can't get past week 2. That means you don't own a business — you own a job that pays you what a business would.

Process documentation is what flips you from employee to owner. The instant you can hand a process to a VA or new hire and they execute without you in the loop, you've made an asset. Until then, every client you add is more work for you, not more leverage.

⚠ EMPLOYEE OF YOUR BUSINESS
No documented processes
  • Every new client = more hours for you
  • Can't take a 2-week vacation without things breaking
  • VA asks the same 5 questions every week
  • Hiring = 3 months of training (most quit before month 2)
  • Selling the agency = impossible (there's nothing to sell)
✓ ACTUAL OWNER
Documented in ClickUp + AI
  • New client = a VA runs through the SOP
  • 2-week vacation = everything runs without you
  • VAs onboard in a week, execute by day 10
  • Quality stays consistent because the process IS the quality
  • Agency is now a sellable asset, not a glorified freelance gig

🚨 The trap most owners fall into: They try to document everything at once. Wrong. Start with the 3 highest-frequency, lowest-judgment tasks — the ones a smart 20-year-old could run with the right doc. Strategy and sales calls come LAST.

🧬 2. The Anatomy of a Real SOP — 4 Components

Every SOP that actually works has these 4 components. Miss one and the process breaks the moment you hand it off.

⚡
Component 1
Trigger
When does this process start? What event kicks it off? Without a trigger, the SOP just sits there.
New client signs contract
→ Run "Client Onboarding" SOP
👤
Component 2
Owner
Who runs this? A role, not a person. "VA" or "Account Manager" — never "Bob." People leave. Roles stay.
Owner: Account Manager
(escalate to Strategist if X)
📋
Component 3
Steps
Numbered. Action-verb-led. Each step has time estimate + the "common mistake" to avoid. Screenshots or Loom embeds where it matters.
1. Log into client's Meta BM (2 min)
⚠ Use agency email, NOT client's
✅
Component 4
Done Definition
3-5 specific checkboxes proving completion. Without this, "done" means whatever the VA decides — and that's how things fall through.
☐ Pixel firing on landing page
☐ Test campaign live in Draft mode
☐ Slack #clients posted

Bad SOP vs. Real SOP

⚠ WHAT MOST OWNERS WRITE
Onboard the client. Get their ad account access, set up the pixel, run a test campaign, let them know it's done. If there's any issue, message me.
✓ WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS
Trigger: Contract signed in HelloSign
Owner: Account Manager
Steps:
1. Send onboarding form (5 min) — template "Client Intake v3"
2. Request BM access (2 min) — use copy-paste msg in Doc
3. Install pixel via GTM (15 min) — Loom: gtm-pixel.loom.com
4. Run test conversion (3 min) — must fire within 30 sec
5. Post in #clients (1 min)
Done:
☐ Pixel firing
☐ Test conversion logged
☐ #clients message posted

📁 Folder: Client Delivery

📄 Weekly Reporting
📄 Campaign Setup
📄 Monthly Optimization Review
📁 Folder: Sales
📄 Lead Qualification
📄 Discovery Call Framework
📄 Proposal Generation
📁 Folder: Operations
📄 Hiring + Trial Project
📄 Payroll & Contractor Pay

💡 Why ClickUp specifically: Docs, Tasks, Templates, and Automations all live in the same workspace. You can convert any step in a Doc into a real Task with one click. Templates let you re-run the same SOP for every new client. Automations fire when triggers happen. Google Docs can't do any of that — you'd need 4 different tools.

🤖 4. The AI Workflow — Write SOPs in 40 Min Instead of 3 Hours

This is the killer move. You don't write the SOP from scratch — you record yourself doing the process once, feed the transcript to AI, and AI writes the SOP. Your job is 5 minutes of cleanup at the end.

Step 1
🎥
Record (Loom)
Do the process once. Talk through it. 5-15 min.
→
Step 2
📝
Transcribe
Loom auto-transcribes. Copy the text.
→
Step 3
⚡
Run the Prompt
Paste into Claude. Use the prompt below. Wait 30 sec.
→
Step 4
📋
Paste to ClickUp
Cleanup 5 min. SOP live.
⚠ THE OLD WAY
~3 hrs
Sit down, stare at blank doc, type from memory, miss half the steps, finish at midnight
✓ THE AI WAY
~40 min
10 min Loom + 30 sec AI + 5 min cleanup. Most of it is the doing-it-once part.

📋 The Prompt (Copy + Paste into Claude)

COPY ALLROLE / CONTEXT: You're helping me convert a Loom recording transcript into a polished SOP for my agency's ClickUp workspace. The reader is a future hire or VA who has never done this task. They're smart but new — don't assume context. GOAL: Turn the transcript below into a clean, structured SOP doc ready to paste into ClickUp Docs. CONSTRAINTS: - Use ONLY information from the transcript — don't invent steps - If the transcript skips a step or detail, mark it [GAP — fill in] in the output - No fluff. No "let's dive in." No corporate AI phrases. - Output as markdown, ready to paste into ClickUp Docs. INPUTS: Process name: [e.g. "Weekly Client Reporting"] Owner role: [e.g. "Account Manager" or "VA"] Transcript: """ [paste full Loom transcript here] """ OUTPUT FORMAT: Markdown with these sections, in this order: # [Process Name] ## Trigger 1 sentence. When does this start? ## Owner 1 sentence. Role, not a person. ## Tools & Access Needed Bullet list of every tool/account needed before starting. ## Steps Numbered list. For each step: - **Action** (verb-led, 1 sentence) - **Time estimate** (rough — 2 min, 5 min, etc.) - **Common mistake** (only if obvious from transcript) - Sub-bullets for any clicks/values needed ## Done Definition 3-5 checkboxes the runner can tick to confirm completion. ## Notes / Edge Cases Anything that doesn't fit elsewhere. EVALUATION: Before answering, confirm: 1. Every step starts with an action verb 2. No corporate AI phrases (unlock, leverage, dive in, etc.) 3. Time estimates included on each step 4. Done Definition has 3-5 specific checkboxes 5. Any transcript gaps marked [GAP — fill in]

🏆 Why this works: The 7-part prompt structure (from Lesson 5.0) forces AI to write in YOUR voice, follow YOUR ClickUp format, and self-check before answering. No revisions. No "make it shorter." First pass = paste-ready 90% of the time.
