🎯 What You're About to Build

By the end of this module you'll have software watching your ads while you sleep, Claude writing copy for you in seconds, and a daily routine that takes maybe 5 minutes. I use this exact setup for my clients.

🔄 Before AI vs. After AI

❌ Before AI
You manually check ads every few hours
You guess which headline works best
You spend 2 hours writing ad copy
You forget to kill a bad ad and waste $200 overnight
✅ After AI
Software watches your ads every minute
AI writes 20 headlines in 30 seconds — you pick the best
Bad ads get killed automatically
You wake up to a report, not a disaster

🏗️ The 2-Tool System

You're setting up two tools that work together:

🧠
Tool 1: Claude Code = The Brain
Writes your ad copy, researches competitors, builds strategy. It's basically a really smart intern that never sleeps.
⚡
Tool 2: The Software = The Hands
Actually creates ads on Facebook, monitors performance, makes changes. The thing that clicks buttons so you don't have to.

You're still the one making decisions. These just do the grunt work.

✅ What You'll Have by the End of Module 5

🧠
Claude Code set up and talking to you
🔗
Software connected to your Facebook ad account
🤖
An AI agent (a set of rules) that manages your ads
⏱️
A daily routine that takes 5 minutes
